About me

Hi!

I'm Roger van Hout. Originally a conceptual artist, with an emphasis on new media. From the beginning of times, I'm messing around with computers. At first, the C64 , later the A1200 and eventually Intels and Macs. Making music (yes, you can find me on the HVSC) and messing around with the video capabilities of the A1200, making games and animations using animated gifs, html flash and now, javascript.

I live in a small village near Groningen with my wife Ruth. We live together with our dog Oki, cats Yolo and Arafat and bunnies Lily and Severus. We have a garden full of fruit and edible flowers. I'm a highly creative, apparently gifted introvert who likes to learn, play and discover new things. Besides coding, I enjoy cooking and I try to learn as much as I can about the Japanese Washoku kitchen. I also enjoy woodworking and I'm in the process of building a wooden strip-plank kayak.

In my free time, I enjoy playing strategic boardgames and I sometimes play Go at the Go- club in Groningen. I'm also fond of windsurfing, cycling, motorcycles and photography. Besides my interests, I'm volunteering for the IHBV, the dutch institute for gifted adults, organizing lectures and meetings for gifted adults.
